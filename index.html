about

Audiovisual artist with a solid background in engineering and a strong inclination towards visual and sound experimentation. He has participated in numerous events and festivals, in Italy and abroad, with AV performances and art exhibitions, collaborating with SR60 (Giuseppe Mascia). His experience includes performances at venues such as LCF and LPM, and various independent underground festivals like Modern Bon (Berlin), as well as in clubs as VJ, with the moniker p#, for international DJs and independent bands. As music producer, he recently released for Macroterra Records in collaboration with label owner Angelo Pomposo. He is currently active with Vesuvian Sighs AV Live in various formats. His artistic practice integrates a wide and varied set of hardware and software, combining a technical approach with an experimental vision.
